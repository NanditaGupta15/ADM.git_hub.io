About ADM

ADM Education & Welfare Society is dedicated to creating positive change through education, skill development, and community engagement. With a strong focus on education and welfare, we aim to empower individuals from all walks of life to realize their full potential. Our multifaceted initiatives span environmental conservation, rural development, vocational training, women's empowerment, health awareness, and support for higher studies.
