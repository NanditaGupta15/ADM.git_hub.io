Anita Devi Memorial Education and Welfare Society

ADM Education & Welfare Society stands as a beacon of hope, dedicated to Empowering Change and Transforming Lives through its multifaceted initiatives. At the heart of our organization lies an unwavering commitment to create positive and lasting impacts, driven by fresh perspectives and innovative ideas.

Rooted in the belief that change starts with education, ADM Society focuses on holistic development across various sectors. Our initiatives encompass a wide spectrum of areas, ranging from Environmental Sustainability and Rural Development to Women's Empowerment and Health Awareness. Through Vocational Training, we equip individuals with practical skills that enhance employability, fostering economic growth at grassroots levels. Moreover, our Higher Education Support program aims to uplift aspiring minds by providing access to quality education.

About ADM

ADM Education & Welfare Society is dedicated to creating positive change through education, skill development, and community engagement. With a strong focus on education and welfare, we aim to empower individuals from all walks of life to realize their full potential. Our multifaceted initiatives span environmental conservation, rural development, vocational training, women's empowerment, health awareness, and support for higher studies.
