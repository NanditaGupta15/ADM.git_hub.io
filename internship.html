Anita Devi Memorial Education and Welfare Society

ADM Education & Welfare Society stands as a beacon of hope, dedicated to creating positive change through education, skill development, and community engagement. With an unwavering commitment, we have conducted 350+ free medical camps, organized numerous plantation drives, facilitated hundreds of free career counseling sessions, and embarked on countless other initiatives. These efforts represent our dedication to transforming lives with fresh perspectives.

In addition to our various outreach programs, we are delighted to provide diverse internship opportunities, offering individuals the chance to gain hands-on experience and contribute meaningfully to society.

INTERNSHIP OPPORTUNITIES:

Our internship opportunities cover a broad spectrum of fields to cater to the diverse talents and interests of our participants. These include:

Management:

  1. Human Resources
  2. Project Manager
  3. CSR Intern
  4. Marketing
  5. Public Relations
  6. Fundraising

Technical:

  1. Graphic Designer
  2. UX/UI Designer
  3. Full Stack Developer
  4. Video Editor

Volunteering:

  1. Medical Volunteer
  2. Campus Ambassador
  3. Charity Intern

Social Media & Marketing:

  1. Social Media Marketing Intern
  2. Anchor
Job description:

PROJECT MANAGER:

  1. In accomplishment of the particular project by building and developing project team and providing directions and motivation to them.
  2. To identify the scope, budget, schedules, risk etc involved in a particular project.
  3. Perform any other duties assigned to them.
  4. Develop a plan to be executed to achieve the target and monitor and track the same.

GRAPHIC DESIGNER:

  1. To create visual concepts using computer softwares like a Adobe Photoshop etc to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, and captivate consumers or other users.
  2. Create visual elements such as logos, original images, and illustrations to produce high-quality designs.
  3. Provide creative inputs,ideas to other departments for design concepts,layouts, visual contents etc.

MEDICAL VOLUNTEER:

  1. As a Medical Volunteer, you will provide essential support and assistance in medical facilities, clinics, or outreach programs. Your role involves offering medical care, aiding healthcare professionals, and contributing to the well-being of patients in diverse healthcare settings.
  2. Spreading awareness about the health issues prevailing in the society.
  3. Provide compassionate care and support to patients under the guidance of medical staff.In addition ,assist the healthcare professionals in administering treatments, performing medical procedures, and conducting health assessments.

VIDEO EDITOR:

  1. Proper editing of the Video i.e. in a creative manner so that the same can be published on the social platforms.
  2. Edit and assemble raw video footage, ensuring logical sequencing and smooth running.
  3. Review and select shots based on the quality, composition, and content relevance.
  4. Trim footage segments and put together the sequence of the film.
  5. Input music, dialogues, graphics, and effects.

UI/UX DESIGNER:

  1. Good at making user interface
  2. Collecting user requirements
  3. Evaluating research
  4. Deliver Outstanding User Experience through designs

MARKETING:

  1. Contribution in ideas
  2. Conducting marketing campaigns
  3. Adapting good strategies
  4. Conducting research

CAMPUS AMBASSADOR:

  1. Leadership skills
  2. Promoting our organization on ground
  3. Ideas for events& campaigns
  4. Collaboration with good firms & collages

CHARITY INTERN:

    Communication Skills
  1. Approach to businesses
  2. Encourage donations
  3. Helping in fundraising activities

FULL STACK DEVELOPERS:

  1. The team is divided into 2 parts FRONTEND DEVELOPERS and BACKEND DEVELOPERS.
  2. The frontend developers have to create user- friendly and responsive websites using HTML,CSS,and JavaScript. And handling user interaction using forms and animations.
  3. The backend developers have to build API to support web applications and manage data.
  4. Along with this communication, testing, debugging, database management are the other important roles to be performed.

Anchor

  1. Create and deliver presentation and coordinating events.
  2. >Maintain a positive attitude and professionalism at all times.
  3. Keep the audience enthusiastic and make the program as joyful as possible.
  4. Should have good Communication skills.

Public Relations

  1. Writing and producing presentations, articles, press releases and media posts.
  2. Dealing with enquiries from the public and press.
  3. Speaking publicly at press conference.
  4. Analysing Media coverage.

Human Resources

  1. Support the Development and implementation of HR initiatives.
  2. Provide counselling on policies and procedures.
  3. Create and implement effective onboarding plans.
  4. Develop training and Development programs.

CSR

  1. Identifying, measuring and monitoring the social values/social impact of the organization's activities.
  2. Influencing the organization's culture by identifying CSR initiatives.
  3. Engaging with external bodies such as charities and community groups.

These internship roles are designed to empower students, housewives, and individuals from all backgrounds to develop valuable skills and gain practical experience. We believe in providing opportunities for personal growth and meaningful contributions to society.


The certificates we distribute upon completion of these internships represent not only a milestone in your personal and professional journey but also your active role in shaping a brighter future for yourself and the community.


Join us in this incredible journey of knowledge, growth, and empowerment, whether through our certificate programs or by participating in our dynamic internships. Your achievements and contributions are our inspiration, and together, we can work towards shaping a more educated, empowered, and capable world.
