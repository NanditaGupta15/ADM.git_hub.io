Our Work

Medical camps

Your health is our priority. ADM Educational and Welfare Society is committed to raising awareness about critical medical issues and empowering individuals to make informed decisions about their health. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare and that education is essential for improving health outcomes. Join us in our mission to build a healthier community!

Plantations

Plant a tree, plant hope. ADM Educational and Welfare Society is committed to protecting our environment and promoting sustainable development. Our plantation drives help to increase green cover, improve air quality, and combat climate change. We believe that every tree makes a difference and that we all have a role to play in protecting our planet.

Join us for our next plantation drive and help us to create a greener and healthier future for all. Or, donate a tree to be planted on your behalf.

Western World Visa Services Pvt. Ltd.

Your dreams start here. ADM Educational and Welfare Society provides comprehensive IELTS preparation programs and abroad study counselling services to help you achieve your academic and professional goals. We believe that everyone has the potential to succeed abroad and that we all deserve the opportunity to pursue our dreams.
